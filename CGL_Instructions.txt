The test duration is 15 minutes.
Candidates must type a passage consisting of approximately 2,000 key depressions.
Candidates must meet the error limits based on their category.
Retyping the passage is not allowed, but candidates can use the remaining time to correct errors.
Use the Tab key to start a new paragraph.
Ensure to insert one space after each punctuation mark.
Any irrelevant matter or numbers typed will be considered mistakes.
Candidates must type the test passage in the medium they have opted for.
Any incomplete word due to any reason will be treated as one full mistake.
