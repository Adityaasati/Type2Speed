b. Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your use of the Site, such as:

  • IP address

  • Pages visited on the Site

We use cookies to enhance user experience on the Site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us analyze how users interact with the Site. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features of the Site may not function properly without them.

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:
